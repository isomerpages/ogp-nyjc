<p>For students pursuing degrees in Science, Engineering, Medicine, Information
and Computer Systems, science at H2 level provides a foundation for their
undergraduate studies.</p>
<p>For students pursuing other degrees, science, even at the H1 level, gives
them an understanding of the principles and limitations of the various
sciences.</p>
