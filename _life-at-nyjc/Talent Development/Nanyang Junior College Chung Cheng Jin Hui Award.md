<p>Nanyang Junior College believes in allowing students to pursue their passion
and it allows students opportunities to engage in student-initiated learning
(SIL) in a unique and empowering manner. The Nanyang Junior College Chung
Cheng Jin Hui award aims to inspire our students to pursue their passions
during their time in NYJC by serving their college and community.</p>
<p>Every year, the award recognises JC2 students who have demonstrated outstanding
achievements in student-initiated projects or programmes - both individual
and team-based - in the areas of CCA, Service-Learning, Academic Research
and Service to the College.</p>
<p>The criteria for the award is as follows:</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; NYJC JC2 students
<br>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Good character and leadership
skills
<br>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Outstanding achievements
in any of the following domains:
<br>o&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Arts
<br>o&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Environment
<br>o&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Community Service
<br>o&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Governance and Society
<br>o&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Science and Technology
<br>o&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Sports and Well-being</p>
