<p>Release of JAE posting results: 3 February 2026
<br><strong>Orientation Dates</strong>: 4, 5, 6 and 9 February 2026</p>
<p>Welcome to Nanyang Junior College! Orientation 2026 is designed to help
our incoming NYJCians settle in, make new friends, and begin their NYJC
journey with confidence.
<br>
<br>What You Need to Know:
<br>
<br>1. Communication of Details :
<br>• On 3 February for student: Orientation Group Leaders (OGLs) will contact
JAE students to share key Orientation details, including reporting arrangements
and administrative follow-ups. Please look out for their phone call in
the afternoon.
<br>• From 4 February for parents/guardians: Key information will be shared
via the Parents Gateway app.
<br>
<br>2. <strong>Reporting on Day 1 (4 February 2026)</strong>
</p>
<p>• Reporting Time: 7.30am
<br>• Venue: NYJC School Hall
<br>• Attire: Secondary school PE attire
<br>• Dismissal (Day 1): By 5.30pm
<br>
<br>3. What Students Should Bring (Day 1)</p>
<p>• Water bottle
<br>• Writing materials
<br>• Personal medication (if any)
<br>• Cash for lunch (exact amount preferred; this amount will be advised
by OGLs)
<br>
<br>4. Orientation Information &amp; Administration (from 3 February onwards)</p>
<p>From 3 February, students will be guided by their OGLs on:
<br>• Accessing the NYJC Portal and Orientation information repository
<br>• Orientation programme overview
<br>• Uniform and school apparel purchases
<br>• Subject information, diagnostic tests (where applicable), and subject
selection
<br>• Lunch arrangements during Orientation</p>
