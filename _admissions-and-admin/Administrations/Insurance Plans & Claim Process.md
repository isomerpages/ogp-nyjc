<p><strong>1.1 MOE Group Personal Accident Insurance for Students</strong>
</p>
<p>All NYJCians are covered under the MOE GPA Insurance Plan, offering comprehensive
24-hour accident coverage worldwide. Students are covered during their
time on school premises, while commuting, and when participating in school-related
activities including Co-Curricular Activities (CCA) and sports, both locally
and internationally.</p>

</p>
<p>Parents should use the online platform for submitting accident insurance
claims and checking their application status.
