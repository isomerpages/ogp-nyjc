<p>Students are required to submit the below to the General Office:</p>
<p>1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Photocopy of existing student pass
(front &amp; back)</p>
<p>2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Write down your handphone number,
email address and whether you are a scholar or international student at
the back of the photocopy student pass</p>
<p>3.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The office will inform you via
email the SOLAR+ Registration Acknowledgement Letter (RAL) at a later date</p>
<p>4.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Student to proceed to complete
the eform application in the ICA website <a href="https://eservices.ica.gov.sg/ipsolarplus/web/index" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">SOLARPLUS</a> upon
receiving the RAL.</p>
<p>Students may approach the General Office for assistance.</p>
